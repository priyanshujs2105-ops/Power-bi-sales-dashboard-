📌 Project Overview
This Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business sales performance by analyzing key metrics such as total sales, total orders, units sold, product performance, salesperson contribution, payment preferences, category distribution, and regional sales trends.
The dashboard helps stakeholders quickly understand sales patterns, identify high-performing areas, and make data-driven business decisions.
🎯 Objective
The main objective of this dashboard is to:
Track overall sales performance through key KPIs.
Analyze monthly sales trends and identify growth patterns.
Compare salesperson performance.
Understand customer purchasing behavior through payment modes.
Identify top-performing products and categories.
Evaluate regional sales contribution.
📊 Key Dashboard Insights
KPI Cards
Total Sales: Displays overall revenue generated.
Total Orders: Shows the number of completed orders.
Total Units Sold: Represents total product quantity sold.
Sales Trend Analysis
Monthly Sales Trend: Tracks sales fluctuations over different months to identify peak and low-performing periods.
Salesperson Performance
Compares sales contribution by each salesperson to identify top performers and areas needing improvement.
Category Analysis
Shows sales distribution between product categories such as Electronics and Furniture.
Product Performance
Highlights products generating the highest revenue and helps identify best-selling products.
Payment Mode Analysis
Analyzes customer payment preferences (UPI, Card, Cash, EMI) to understand transaction behavior.
Regional Performance
Compares sales across regions to identify strong and weak markets.
🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
Power BI – Dashboard development and visualization
Microsoft Excel – Data cleaning and preparation
DAX – Creating calculated measures and KPIs
Power Query – Data transformation and modeling
📈 Business Value
This dashboard enables businesses to:
Monitor sales performance efficiently.
Optimize product and regional strategies.
Improve sales team performance.
Understand customer payment behavior.
Support faster and more informed decision-making.
